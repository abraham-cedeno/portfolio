About the project:

Welcome to the marketing campaign for the pastry roller. In this project, I worked for Loquieropty as a digital marketing analyst. The objective of this project was to create a campaign with a minimum ROAS of 4:1.

Carrying out this project involved multiple activities, including: identifying target audiences; market research; campaign video design; A/B testing design and analysis; customer and lead behavior analysis; landing page design; advertising through Facebook Ads Manager; measuring and optimizing campaign ROI.

About Loquieropty

Loquieropty is a company that sells products through e-commerce in Panama. They have products in various niches such as home, pets, cars, etc. The objective of Loquieropty is to achieve high conversion traffic through PPC campaigns.



Business task

The purpose of this project was to create a campaign with a high level of engagement and high conversion rate that generates a minimum ROAS of 4:1.

Actions taken

  1. Identifying target audiences: Using data to identify and understand the interests and demographic characteristics of the audience.
  2. Market research: Studying campaigns for this product in other markets such as the USA. This examined how the product was positioned and determined a strategy to further improve its positioning.
  3. Designing the campaign video: Once the strategy was determined, a video was created through Adobe Premier Pro, which was later used to promote the product.
  4. Designing the landing page: The landing page for the product was created taking into account the unique value proposition of the product and its positioning before the audience.
  5. Conceptualization of copywriting: Then, the copywriting was created, taking into consideration storytelling principles that were crucial to connect with the audience and achieve a high level of engagement.
  6. Design and analysis of A/B tests: Designing initial tests to examine whether the product could be scaled in the Panamanian market. In these tests, different versions of the campaign were tested, changing factors such as the subject line, the same video, the landing page, etc.
  7. Analysis of customer and lead behavior: Collecting and analyzing customer data to understand their buying habits, behavior on the website, and level of satisfaction.
  8. Advertising with Facebook Ads Manager: After determining a scalable version of the campaign following the small tests, the budget was considerably increased to sell the product in large quantities.
  9. Monitoring and optimizing the campaign's ROI: Finally, the campaign was monitored through recurring reports in Facebook Business Manager and Google Analytics, which allowed for optimal adjustments and a better ROI.
  10. Email marketing and Google Ads: Subsequently, the campaign was complemented with other digital marketing strategies such as email marketing with Klaviyo and Google Ads.



Result

A successful campaign was created with a much higher than expected ROAS of 12.95:1. Other KPIs also yielded very satisfactory results, including CPC, CTR, and engagement.
