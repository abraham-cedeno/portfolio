About the project

Welcome to the article "Beer mugs: Which one is the best in 2023??" In this project, I worked as a digital marketing analyst in SEO for a confidential company, representing the marketing agency Global Commerce Media.

The objective of this project is to create content with SEO strategies. This involved multiple activities, including: market research and analysis; competitor analysis; keyword analysis; and content creation with SEO strategies.

Business task

The purpose of this project is to study the competition in the SERPs for the keyword "Beer mugs" and analyze the data collected from SEO tools to determine the most optimal strategy to follow, and create quality content that attracts organic traffic to the client's website.

Actions taken

  1. Market research: Conducted market research to better understand the audience and their search intent based on data analysis.
  2. Competitor analysis: Collected and analyzed data from competitors appearing in the SERPs for the target keyword. This allowed for informed decision-making to create content with superior quality and meeting the search intent of the audience.
  3. Keyword analysis: Analyzed keywords using tools such as Ahrefs, Google KW Planner, Google Trends, Ubbersuggest, and Answerthepublic, resulting in the creation of more visible content for the client's website.
  4. Creation of content with SEO strategy:Finally, the content was created in a modern way to engage readers, using SEO strategies such as readability, internal link building, and keyword density.



Result

The content created met the quality standards set by the client. It also achieved its goal by appearing in relevant searches and generating organic traffic for the client. Additionally, the content was ensured to be Evergreen so that it remains relevant for a long time.
