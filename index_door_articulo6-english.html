About the project

Welcome to the article "All you need to know about Sprint Backlog". In this project, I worked for the company Gitnux as a digital marketing analyst in SEO representing the marketing agency Global Commerce Media.

The aim of this project was to create content with SEO strategies. It involved multiple activities including market research, competitor analysis, keyword analysis, and content creation with SEO strategies.

About Gitnux

Gitnux is a company that sells the latest trends in software. They have a wide variety of software in industries such as marketing, human resources, and business management. The objective of Gitnux is to increase organic traffic to their website through quality content on their blog.



Business task

The purpose of this project was to study the competition in the SERPs for the keyword "Sprint Backlog" and analyze the collected data from SEO tools to determine the most optimal strategy to follow and achieve quality content that attracts organic traffic to the Gitnux website.

Actions taken

  1. Market research: Conducted market research to better understand the audience (in this particular case, Spain) and their search intent based on data analysis.
  2. Competitor analysis: Collected and analyzed data from competitors that appear in the SERPs for the keyword being targeted. This allowed for informed decision-making to create superior quality content that met the search intent of the audience.
  3. Keyword analysis: Analyzed keywords based on tools such as Ahrefs, Google KW Planner, Google Trends, Ubbersuggest, and Answerthepublic, which resulted in the creation of content with more visibility for the client's website.
  4. Content creation with SEO strategy: Finally, the content was created in a modern way to engage readers and using SEO strategies such as readability, internal link building, and keyword density.



Result

A content that met the quality standards determined by the client was created. It also achieved its goal by appearing in relevant searches and generating organic traffic for Gitnux. Additionally, it was ensured that the content created was evergreen so that it remains relevant for a long time.
