About the project:

Welcome to the article "How to Write a Business Case: Step-by-Step Guide". In this project, I worked for the company Gitnux as a digital marketing analyst in SEO, representing the marketing agency Global Commerce Media.

The goal of this project was to create content using SEO strategies. It involved multiple activities including market research, competitor analysis, keyword analysis, and content creation with SEO strategies.

About Gitnux:

Gitnux is a company that specializes in selling the latest trends in software. They offer a wide range of software in industries such as marketing, human resources, and business management. Gitnux's objective is to increase organic traffic to their website through quality content on their blog.



Business task

The purpose of this project was to study the competition in the SERPs for the keywords "Business Case" and analyze the data collected from SEO tools to determine the most optimal strategy to follow and achieve quality content that attracts organic traffic to Gitnux's website.

Actions taken

  1. Market research: Conducted market research to better understand the audience (in this particular case, Spain) and their search intent based on data analysis.
  2. Competitor analysis: Collected and analyzed data from competitors that appear in the SERPs for the keyword being targeted. This allowed for informed decision-making to create superior quality content that met the search intent of the audience.
  3. Keyword analysis: Analyzed keywords based on tools such as Ahrefs, Google KW Planner, Google Trends, Ubbersuggest, and Answerthepublic, which resulted in the creation of content with more visibility for the client's website.
  4. Content creation with SEO strategy: Finally, the content was created in a modern way to engage readers and using SEO strategies such as readability, internal link building, and keyword density.



Result

We were able to create content that met the quality standards determined by the client. It also achieved its goal of appearing in relevant searches and generating organic traffic for Gitnux. Additionally, we ensured that the created content was Evergreen so that it remains relevant for a long time.
